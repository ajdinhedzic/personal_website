about

Hi folks! I'm Ajdin (pronounced idean). I'm a full stack developer at a Principal Financial Group in Des Moines. You might think a large financial institution means Java 6, XML and cubicles but that's not the case here. It's a great place to work. Outside of work, my interests are in Javascript and Android. I am always looking to improve my knowledge of the front-end world, including UX.
